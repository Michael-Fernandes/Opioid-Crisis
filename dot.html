Dotplots for Decision Making

Making decisions is hard... dot plots can help! On top of giving a sense of the its shape, dot plots present data in a format that allows people to easily group dots together for quick comparisons and decisions. I made a convenient D3 visualization for dot plots that allows a user to input one-dimensional data and generates a dot plot.

App Prototype: Whats the Deal?

To illustrate a use case of dotplots, I did a quick prototype of an app to help shoppers make buying decisions when shopping. I designed an interface that allows users to look up grocery items and compare the prices for a particular item in their local area.
