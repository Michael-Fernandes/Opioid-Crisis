Recovery Assistant

A Patient facing healthcare app to help with pain management after an surgical operation. I took on pro-bono work to make usability fixes and aesthetic changes to make this app more usable and enjoyable.

This was a fun introduction to healthcare design and got me thinking about how to user-centered design without direct access to sensitive patients. Drawing from the wisdom of health are providers is important, but also collecting volunteer feedback and monitoring key metrics is as important.

When doing my design adjustments I did some reading on UCD around healthcare aplpications and was super inspired by a paper called "mHealth for Maternal Mental Health: Everyday Wisdom in Ethical Design". The paper suggests a design process for patient facing healthcare technology. The process centers around care providers, pairing their expertise with the knowledge of UX professionals, before validating a working prototype with real patients.
